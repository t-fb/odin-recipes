Prancing Pineapple Cake

So freakin' good, it'll make you prance the night away!

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 9 slices of pineapple
  • optional: marashino cherries
  • 1 1/3 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup shortening
  • 1 1/2 tsps baking powder
  • 1/2 tsps salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  2. In a sqaure pan, melt the butter in the oven and then sprinkle brown sugar evenly over the melted butter, then arrange the pineapple slices on top of the brown sugar.
  3. In a medium bowl, beat remaining ingredients with an electric mixer on low for 30 seconds, then beat on high for 3 minutes.
  4. Pour batter over the pineapple and cherries.
  5. Bake for 50-55 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!
